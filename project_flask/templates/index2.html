Summary Plot

The summary plot shows the means of rep times for all sets of 10 that have been recorded in our data. Each unit (1) on the x-axis represents 1 set of 10 reps of bicep curls, while each unit (1) on the y-axis represents 1 second in time. The plot will be re-computed upon the receiving of new rep times, and newly computed plot will show after a page refresh.
